01About

Language reasoning, made rigorous.

I am a last-year Ph.D. student in AI at LIRMM, CNRS and the University of Montpellier. My research combines LLMs with logic and linguistic formalisms for natural language inference and automatic contradiction detection.

I am especially interested in reliable language technologies for high-stakes applications, where formal reasoning and careful evaluation matter as much as raw capability.
