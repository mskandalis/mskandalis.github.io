About Me

I am a last-year Ph.D. student in AI at LIRMM, CNRS & University of Montpellier. I am funded by the French Ministry of the Armed Forces through the Defence Innovation Agency (AID) 🪖, and by the Cybersecurity Institute of Occitanie (ICO) 🛡️.

My current research focuses on combining LLMs with logic and linguistic formalisms for the tasks of Natural Language Inference (NLI) and automatic contradiction detection, including in high-stakes application domains.
