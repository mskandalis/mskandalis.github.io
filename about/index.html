Language reasoning, made rigorous.

Profile

A concern for reliable language technology.

I am a last-year Ph.D. student in artificial intelligence at LIRMM, CNRS and the University of Montpellier. My research combines large language models with logic and linguistic formalisms for natural language inference and automatic contradiction detection.

I work toward language technologies that remain legible and dependable in high-stakes settings, where formal reasoning, data quality, and careful evaluation matter as much as raw capability.
